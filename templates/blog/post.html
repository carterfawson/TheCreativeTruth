Tsunami of Information

I just wrote itCarter

So I think that it might be cool to have one post that we can have on here to start out with about what we do and how we turn information into wisdom. Some cool infographic about how we are bombarded with so much information every single day that can be hard to sort through. There are politicians that are constantly making claims and promises. Sports teams that are touted to win the championship. Social stats that are quoted without really any legitimate sources or knowledge of whether or not the correct methods of analysis were used. This past year alone we have seen the emergence of evidence that there have been tens of thousands of academic studies, put in journals and used in policy decisions, that did not use sound research methods and ultimately could not be reproduced. Here at The Creative Truth we want to work towards a more informed society, but even better than that we want to work towards a wiser society. A society that knows the truth, backed by sound data. That's the society that we believe can make a better future.
